Security

Compliance

Quanto® offers a large number of elements to comply with the regulations of SHCP, SAT, CNBV, CNSF, Condusef, among others. Both for Leasing and Factoring companies, SOFOMES, SOFIPOS, SOCAPs, Credit Unions, Fintech and Banks.

Some of these elements include Risk Matrices (EBR), Knowledge of the Client (KYC), Automatic alerts, operation limits. As well as reports that are sent to regulatory entities such as reports of relevant, unusual and worrying internal operations.
