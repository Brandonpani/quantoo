Learn about the modules offered by Quanto®

Quanto® is a modular platform that allows the key activities of institutions to be carried out. Below you will find a description of the functionality of some of its modules and how it is integrated into the value chain.

Explore the modules

Customers

Quanto® is a solution oriented to people and not to clients, i.e. It allows you to register a person and assign a role for each corresponding operation, be it prospect, client, guarantor, guarantor or joint and several obligor. In addition, it requests all the data indicated by the current AML regulations.

Regulatory Compliance

It includes all the fields requested by current AML regulations to identify a client.

Personalization

It is possible to customize the requested fields, documents to scan, documents to print and alerts to generate through the configuration options.

Validation

Automatically validates fields such as Personal Identification, Tax Identification, Bank Accounts, Email, Telephone, among others, with algorithms issued by regulatory entities, as well as the possibility of connecting to regulatory entities for online validation.

Automation​

When saving, it automatically searches for the person in the lists of high-risk people, as well as calculates the risk matrix, assigns the level of risk and generates the documents configured to be printed.

A solution to solve all challenges.

At Quanto® we strive to create a solution that allows you to manage all the key processes of your company.

AML

Quanto® analyzes the risk in real time, automatically searches the names of the clients that are registered in various list providers, assigns the risk classification of the clients based on a preconfigured risk matrix and monitors all the operations carried out on the platform.

Search for people in lists

Automatically searches lists of people linked to AML or PEPS in providers such as: OFAC, Quien es Quien, Lexis Nexis and WorldCheck. It also has options to search individually and massively.

Customer Risk Matrix

It has more than 40 risk factors, for each indicator it is possible to define the type of risk, probability and impact it has. Additionally, it is possible to design several risk matrices that are applied depending on the characteristics of the client.

Alerts

Allows you to configure relevant, unusual, and concerning internal alerts. It is possible to define when the operations are analyzed, either when importing them, registering them, at the end of the month or when carrying out a specific process, as well as modifying the limit amounts of additional fins.

Entity Risk Matrix

It has more than 40 risk factors, for each indicator it is possible to define the type of risk, probability and impact it has. Additionally, it is possible to define attenuators to calculate the real risk of the entity.
