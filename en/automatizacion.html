Automation

Automating allows you to reduce effort and consequently lower operating costs, which is why automating has been a key process for highly competitive teams. Quanto® allows you to automate the entire life cycle of financial products through some of the following options.

Notifications

Quanto® allows users and clients to be notified through different business rules. For example, it is possible to create a notification in the authorization transaction to notify the treasury area by mail or through the message center, it is also possible to create a notification that notifies the client of his next payment, when it is due, on the day of expiration or when a certain amount of days expires.

Automate your key processes

Quanto® automates key processes and increases productivity to help you achieve your strategic goals.

Workflow

The Workflow editor allows you to define the phases that are necessary for an operation to be authorized, that is, the company can establish if a payroll loan only requires going through the control desk, credit committee and authorization stages, while a SME credit requires going through the additional stages of legal review and visual inspection before authorization.

Customize

It is possible to define custom fields, which allows users to make better decisions by being better informed, in addition, companies can edit the formats they generate, for example, if the regulations change it is very easy to add, modify or remove a paragraph from the contract or promissory note , or simply when you want to change the font or font size.
