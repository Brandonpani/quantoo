Explore the products you can manage

Quanto® is a multi-product platform that allows end-to-end operation following financial products. Below you will find the description and functionality of some of its modules.

Continue Exploring

Credit

Quanto® is a flexible financial platform that allows you to manage any type of product such as payroll, personal, automotive, group, mortgage, business and agricultural during the entire credit life cycle.

Types of Credit​

Simple credits can be created as well as revolving and non-revolving checking accounts, at a fixed or variable rate, additionally there are 50 configuration parameters.

Documentation

It allows you to print payment plans, promissory notes and contracts, additionally it allows you to attach digitized documents such identifications, articles of incorporation, proof of address, proof of income or financial statements.

Mass Processes

Credits can be registered, authorized and disbursed in a massive way, In addition, individual subscriptions, massive by file, massive by API, can be applied. It also allows you to massively send expiration notices, account statements and electronic invoices.

Reports

It has a wide set of reports on balances, maturities, payment plans, payment and processes that can be exported to PDF and XLS, plus all reports have filters by client, group, product, currency, official, branch, among others. Additionally, it they can project payment plans of a credit or client, it is even possible to make a projection of income in general, as well as reports to Buro and Credit circle

Lease

is a comprehensive financial platform that allows you to manage pure leasing, financial leasing and renting throughout the life of the lease.

Asset Management

It allows assets management linked to the lease, from its registration, maintenance management, paperwork management, valuations and inspections, as well as the recovery, loss or sale of the asset.

Documentation

It allows you to print payment plans, promissory notes and contracts, additionally it allows you to attach digitized documents such as identifications, articles of incorporation, proof of address and financial statements.

Mass Processes

Leases can be registered, authorized and disbursed in bulk, In addition, individual subscriptions, massive by file, massive by API, can be applied. It also allows you to massively send expiration notices, account statements and electronic invoices.

Reports

It has a wide set of balance reports, maturities, income plans and processes that can be exported to PDF and XLS, in addition all the reports have filters by client, group, product, currency, official, branch, among others. Additionally, rental plans of a lease or client can be projected, it is even possible to make a projection of income in general, as well as reports to the Bureau and Credit Circle.

Factoring

Quanto® is a multi-product financial platform that integrates factoring management for customers and suppliers throughout the factoring life cycle.

Invoice Management

It allows you to manage invoices from the massive import and automatic validation of the invoices in the factoring register to the payment, credit notes and debit notes of the invoices that support the factoring.

Mass Processes

Factoring can be registered, authorized and disbursed in a massive way, additionally individual payments, massive by file, massive by API can be applied, it also allows you to send expiration notices, account statements and electronic invoices in a massive way.

Documentation

It allows you to print payment plans, promissory notes and contracts, additionally it allows you to attach digitized documents such as identifications, articles of incorporation, proof of address or financial statements.

Reports

It has a wide set of balance reports, maturities, income plans and processes that can be exported to PDF and XLS, in addition all the reports have filters by client, group, product, currency, official, branch, among others. Additionally, rental plans of a lease or client can be projected, it is even possible to make a projection of income in general, as well as reports to the Bureau and Credit Circle.
